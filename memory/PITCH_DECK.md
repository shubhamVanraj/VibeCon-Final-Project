## SLIDE 2 — THE PROBLEM

### India's Loan Market is Broken — For Borrowers.

**The numbers:**
- Rs. 38.6 Lakh Crore in outstanding credit (RBI, 2026)
- 2.8 Lakh+ loan applications processed daily
- 10.2 Crore loans disbursed annually (FY 2025-26)

**The experience today:**

| What happens today | Impact |
|---|---|
| You fill a form on BankBazaar / PaisaBazaar | Your data is sold to 10-15 banks instantly |
| Within 5 minutes | 8-12 spam calls from unknown numbers |
| You see "8.5% interest rate" advertised | Actual cost is 12-15% higher after fees |
| You take the first offer you get | Overpay Rs. 5,000 - Rs. 75,000 over loan tenure |
| You want to stop calls | No way to revoke. Data already sold. |

**Real example:**
A salaried professional in Bokaro applies for a Rs. 10 Lakh personal loan.
- BankBazaar: Gets 11 calls in 2 hours from banks they never chose.
- Picks the first bank that calls — pays 11.5% when they could have got 10.49%.
- **Overpays Rs. 38,000 over 5 years.** Never even knew.

> "Existing platforms serve banks. Not borrowers."

## SLIDE 3 — THE INSIGHT

### The Aggregator Business Model is Misaligned

**How current platforms make money:**
1. User fills form with personal details
2. Platform sells the "lead" to 10-15 banks at Rs. 200-500 per lead
3. Banks spam call the user competing for attention
4. Platform gets paid regardless of user experience

**The fundamental problem:**
Their customer is the bank, not you.

**What borrowers actually want:**
1. See ALL options transparently (not just partners)
2. Compare total cost — not just headline rate
3. Control who can contact them
4. Decide at their own pace, not under spam pressure

## SLIDE 4 — THE SOLUTION

### Rinkosh: You Compare. You Choose. You Control.

**How Rinkosh works differently:**

```
STEP 1: Browse & Explore (No signup needed)
→ 56 loan products, 20 banks, 10 categories
→ See interest rates, fees, total cost upfront
→ Filter by category, compare side-by-side

STEP 2: Tell Us About You (2 min onboarding)
→ Loan type, income, credit score, amount needed
→ AI auto-selects if you came from a specific product

STEP 3: Get Personalized Recommendations
→ Ranked by TOTAL COST (not just interest rate)
→ Each has approval probability with REASONS
→ "Good credit score" / "Income below bank minimum"

STEP 4: YOU Choose Who Contacts You
→ Click "I'm Interested" only on banks YOU pick
→ Nobody else gets your data. Period.
→ Revoke access anytime.
```

**Key differentiator: Zero data sharing without explicit user consent.**

## SLIDE 7 — BUSINESS MODEL

### How Rinkosh Makes Money

**Revenue Stream 1: Performance-based Lead Fees**
- Bank pays Rinkosh only when user clicks "I'm Interested"
- Rs. 500-2,000 per qualified, consent-based lead
- Higher quality = higher willingness to pay (user chose the bank, not spam)

**Revenue Stream 2: Commission on Disbursals**
- 0.5-1% of loan amount on successful disbursal
- Rs. 10 Lakh loan = Rs. 5,000-10,000 commission
- Already built: Commission tracking in admin dashboard

**Revenue Stream 3: Premium Bank Listings (Future)**
- Banks pay for featured placement in recommendations
- Clearly labeled as "Promoted" — no loss of trust

**Revenue Stream 4: Credit Score Products (Future)**
- CIBIL/Experian integration — freemium model
- Premium: Detailed credit report + improvement roadmap

**Unit Economics Target:**
- CAC: Rs. 100-200 (organic + content-led growth)
- LTV: Rs. 800-3,000 (lead fee + disbursal commission)
- LTV:CAC ratio: 4-15x
